# 01-product-requirements.md

# Project Name

Vyaa

# Product Vision

Vyaa is a premium women's fashion ecommerce platform focused on delivering a seamless shopping experience for modern Indian women.

The platform should feel elegant, trustworthy, fast, and premium.

Primary goal:

Allow customers to discover, browse, purchase, and manage fashion products online.

Secondary goal:

Provide administrators with complete control over products, inventory, orders, promotions, customers, and analytics.

---

# Target Audience

Women aged 18-45

Primary segments:

* College students
* Working professionals
* Fashion-conscious shoppers
* Ethnic wear buyers
* Casual wear buyers
